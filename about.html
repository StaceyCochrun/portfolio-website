About Me

My Story

I am a UI designer deeply rooted in the beautiful Pacific Northwest, living just outside of Seattle.

I grew up drawing all things horses and realized early on that I had a passion for art and design. This passion ultimately led me to pursue a degree in Marketing and Graphic Design.

I have an extensive background in Graphic Design working in a large variety of industries. This experience and my interest in visual design further led me to UI/UX. So many of my skills have been transferable to UI, and I’ve been able to harness my knowledge and apply that to the branding and styling aspects of my UI/UX designs. Combining my graphic design knowledge with my customer-facing experience has well equipped me in creating simple, clean, pixel-perfect and aesthetically pleasing user-centric interfaces.

I am married with two kids, a dog, and love anything relating to the outdoors. In my spare time, you can typically find me with a cup of coffee in my hand, organizing something. I find deep joy in taking something that is messy and disorganized and turning it into a well functioning system. I believe this odd desire also transfers well over into my UI design skills.
